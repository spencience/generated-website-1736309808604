Fascinating Duck Facts

Amazing Diving Abilities

Some ducks can dive up to 240 feet deep! They have special adaptations that allow them to stay underwater for up to a minute.

Sleep with One Eye Open

Ducks can sleep with one half of their brain while the other half stays alert for potential dangers!

Waterproof Feathers

Ducks produce special oil from a gland that they spread over their feathers to make them waterproof.

Global Travelers

Some duck species migrate over 3,000 miles twice a year! They can fly at speeds up to 70 mph.

Built-in Filters

Ducks have special filters in their bills called lamellae that help them filter food from water and mud.

Temperature Control

Ducks have a special blood vessel system in their legs that helps them regulate their body temperature in cold water.

Did You Know?

A duck's quack doesn't echo! Although this is actually a myth, it's one of the most commonly believed duck "facts."
