Duck-Friendly Whole Grain Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup cracked corn
  • 1/4 cup peas (thawed if frozen)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Combine warm water and yeast, let stand for 5 minutes
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Add honey and yeast mixture to dry ingredients
  4. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth
  5. Let rise for 1 hour
  6. Shape into small pieces suitable for ducks
  7. Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes

Duck Feeding Tips

  • ✓ Feed in small portions
  • ✓ Break into tiny pieces
  • ✓ Ensure bread floats
  • ✓ Feed alongside natural foods
  • ✓ Don't overfeed

Duck Nutritionist's Note

This recipe is specially formulated to be nutritious and safe for ducks. Remember that bread should only be an occasional treat and not a primary food source for our feathered friends.
